# Playful Image Resources for Wordy

## Recommended Sources (CC0/Public Domain)

### 1. OpenClipart.org
- License: CC0 (Public Domain)
- Search for: "cute [word]", "cartoon [word]", "playful [word]"
- Format: SVG
- URL: https://openclipart.org/

### 2. FreeSVG.org
- License: CC0 (Public Domain)
- Has many playful cartoon images
- URL: https://freesvg.org/

### 3. SVGSilh.com
- License: CC0
- Good cartoon collection
- URL: https://svgsilh.com/

### 4. PublicDomainVectors.org
- License: Public Domain
- Cartoon animals and objects
- URL: https://publicdomainvectors.org/

## Search Keywords for Each Word

For better playful results, search with these modifiers:
- "cute cartoon [word]"
- "kawaii [word]"
- "playful [word]"
- "children [word]"
- "smiling [word]"

## Tips
1. Look for images with faces/expressions (anthropomorphized)
2. Prefer bright, vibrant colors
3. Choose simple, clear designs (not too detailed)
4. Avoid realistic/photographic styles
5. Look for images that are fun and engaging for toddlers
